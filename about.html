ABOUT ME

Environmental scientist in formation. Evidence-led by nature.

I'm Adnin Huraiyah, an environmental science student at Universiti Malaysia Kelantan. Watching the quiet collapse of ecosystems, one incident after another, is what pulled me toward this path. It was not simply concern; it became a conviction that prevention alone is not enough.

My interest does not stop at understanding what causes environmental damage. I want to trace it, analyse it, and turn it into knowledge that can guide action. Knowing the cause is only half the work; doing something with that knowledge is the rest.

This is not merely a field of study to me. It is a responsibility I have chosen to carry: to protect what remains and to fight for what can still be restored.
